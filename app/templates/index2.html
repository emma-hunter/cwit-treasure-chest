What are Treasure Chest Points?

The Center for Women in Technology (CWIT) has a Treasure Chest program that allows you to attend events and other CWIT initiatives to earn points towards prizes. Every CWIT event members attend, they will earn treasure chest points for. These points can latter be traded in for various prizes at the end of the semester.
